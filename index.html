Plot my electrical (kwh) Usage

Data pulled in (csv) from file, and presented graphically

The calendar layout is from this D3 example. I adapted the code to chew on my data and also allow for multiple criteria for the display.

Mouse-over individual boxes for a summation of the day.

White boxes indicate no data. The darker the box, the higher the kwh usage of that day. The first graph shows totals of all electrical usage that day.
