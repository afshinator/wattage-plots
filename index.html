PlotWatt Data Visualization Project

Graphs of energy usage data from Plotwatt.com csv file - by Afshin Mokhtari

Use the stacked Bar Chart to see

  • the range of usage amounts per day,
  • the relative contributions of each appliance type.

The Calendar Heat Maps gives you quick answers to:

  • how much does the usage vary?
  • when are the peak/low usage dates?
  • what are the patterns for peak/low dates?
