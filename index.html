Electrical (kwh) Usage Visualizations

of data from PlotWatt file

The Calendar Heat Maps below (inspired by D3 example) gives you quick answers to:

  • how much does the usage vary?
  • when are the peak/low usage dates?
  • what are the patterns for peak/low dates?
The first one shows a total of all usage, per day. The next 6 show total usage per day of individual appliance types.

The Stacked Bar Chart helps you see the range of usage amounts per day, and also the relative contribuations of each appliance type.

Each Box represents one day. The darker the color of the box, the higher the usage. Mouse-over boxes to see actual numbers.
